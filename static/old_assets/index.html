How It Works

Three simple steps from classroom to kitchen wall

01

BMI Health Assessment

Teacher measures the student's BMI. Underweight or overweight classifications are dynamically mapped to customized portion needs.

Try BMI Tracker
02

AI Generates Meal Poster

Rule engine optimizes and creates a balanced, zero-repeat, highly local weekly food chart under ₹50 per meal.

Try Meal Generator
03

Print or Share Meal Plan

Teachers print the poster or share via WhatsApp. Parents scan the QR code for full recipes in English and Kannada.

Project NutriPrint

Problem Statement: Lack of awareness and training among parents regarding healthy eating habits for children.

Parents in rural and semi-urban Karnataka lack systematic, easily accessible awareness about balanced child nutrition. NutriPrint solves this by routing customized dietary printouts through school teachers — reaching the kitchen wall every morning, requiring zero technology adoption from parents.
