Why NutriPrint

A platform built around real school challenges

Identifying the problem is the first step. NutriPrint closes the gap with targeted digital tools.

⚠️ Challenges
  • 😟 Childhood malnutrition and micronutrient deficiencies in school-going children
  • 🍽️ Poor food awareness — children and parents unaware of balanced nutritional needs
  • 📋 Lack of structured nutrition guidance for teachers without clinical training
  • 💸 Difficulty planning balanced, affordable meals using locally available regional foods
✅ NutriPrint Solutions
  • 📐 BMI Assessment — age and gender-specific classification with personalised advice
  • 🤖 AI Nutrition Advisor — Groq-powered bilingual guidance in English and Kannada
  • 🗓️ Weekly Meal Planning — regional, budget-aware 7-day plans generated in seconds
  • 📚 Nutrition Education — printable posters and QR recipes to educate parents and students

Real-World Impact

How NutriPrint Creates Impact

From a single teacher assessment to a healthier child — here's the full journey.

👩‍🏫
Step 1
Teacher Assessment
A classroom teacher enters the student's age, gender, height, and weight — no clinical training needed. Takes under a minute.
📊
Step 2
BMI Analysis
NutriPrint calculates the BMI using WHO age-gender percentile standards and instantly classifies the student as Underweight, Normal, Overweight, or Obese.
🤖
Step 3
AI Nutrition Advisor
Groq-powered AI answers the teacher's nutrition questions in English or Kannada before the plan is generated — helping build dietary understanding.
🍽️
Step 4
Personalized Meal Plan
A complete 7-day meal plan is generated using regional Karnataka foods, staying within a ₹150/day budget and matching the student's age group and diet type.
🖨️
Step 5
Nutrition Poster
The plan is exported as a printable nutrition poster with nutrient breakdowns, food visuals, and the student's health summary — ready to go home with the child.
📱
Step 6
QR Recipe Access
Every meal on the poster has a QR code. Parents scan it on any smartphone to instantly open step-by-step recipes using ingredients available in their local market.
👨‍👩‍👧
Step 7
Parent Awareness
Parents receive clear, actionable guidance in their language — understanding what to cook, how much to serve, and why each food matters for their child's growth.
🥗
Outcome
Healthier Children
Consistent, nutritious meals lead to better energy, improved concentration in class, stronger immunity, and healthier growth — the real-world outcome NutriPrint is built for.
